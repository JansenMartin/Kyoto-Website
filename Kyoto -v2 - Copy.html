Wander through a City Steeped in History

Kyoto

Kyoto is one of the oldest cities in Japan and was once the Japanese imperial capital. Visitors will find an incredible number of temples and shrines, exquisite seasonal foods, and serene landscapes that tell a different story with every season.

A Story for Every Season

Kyoto is full of gorgeous natural scenery for every season. Winter, Spring, Summer or Fall...visitors flock from all around the world to experience these four facets of Kyoto. Based on the time of year, here are my top recommendations:

  • Autumn leaves at Kiyomimizu-dera (mid-Nov. to early-Dec.)
  • Watching snow fall at Kinkaku-ji (early Dec. to mid-Mar.)
  • Plum blossoms at Kitano Tenmangu Shrine (Feb.)
  • Geisha dance performance at Miyako Odori (April)
  • Riverside dining (Jun. to Sept.)
